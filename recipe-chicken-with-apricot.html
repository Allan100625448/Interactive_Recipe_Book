Dinner: Chicken With Apricot

Click For Larger Image

Ingredients

  1. 3 Red Royale potatoes
  2. 2 tbs olive oil
  3. 1 garlic clove, crushed
  4. 1 tsp smoked paprika
  5. 4 Coles RSPCA Approved Chicken Breast Fillets
  6. 410g can apricot halves in juice, drained, finely chopped
  7. ¼ cup (35g) pistachios, toasted, finely chopped
  8. ⅓ cup finely chopped flat-leaf parsley
  9. 1 long red chilli, seeded, finely chopped
  10. 1 tbs lime juice
  11. Mixed salad leaves, to serve

Method

  1. Step 1: Place the potatoes on a microwave-safe plate between 2 pieces of paper towel. Cook in microwave on high (100%) for 4 mins or until just tender. Thickly slice.
  2. Step 2: Combine oil and garlic in a bowl. Toss the potato and half the oil mixture in a bowl. Stir paprika into remaining oil mixture. Rub over chicken. Season.
  3. Step 3: Heat a barbecue grill or chargrill on medium-high. Cook chicken for 3-4 mins each side or until cooked through.
  4. Step 4: Meanwhile, cook the potato on grill for 2 mins each side or until golden.
  5. Step 5: Combine apricot, pistachio, parsley, chilli and lime juice in a bowl. Season. Spoon over chicken. Serve with potato and salad leaves.
